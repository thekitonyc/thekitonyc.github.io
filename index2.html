The Ki to NYC

Hi, I’m Ki! From the moment I could hold a pencil, I’ve been sketching my dreams of a life in New York City, surrounded by the energy and creativity that the fashion world has to offer. Fashion isn’t just a passion for me; it’s a way to tell stories, celebrate individuality, and spread joy through art. I believe that clothing has the power to transform how we see ourselves and the world around us.

Every time I see a beautiful outfit or a stunning runway show, my heart races with excitement and inspiration. I’ve poured my heart into honing my skills through classes, community workshops, and countless late-night projects. But to truly chase my dreams, I need to study in NYC, where the heartbeat of fashion thrives.

However, this journey comes with its challenges, and I can’t do it alone. I’m humbly reaching out for donations to help cover my tuition and living expenses while I embark on this incredible adventure. Your support, no matter how big or small, means the world to me and will bring me one step closer to my dreams.

Thank you for considering helping me turn my dreams into reality. Together, we can make a difference!
